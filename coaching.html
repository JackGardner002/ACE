Coaching

JJJJ is the coach for the ACE Knight's team. There are also other individual coaches, contact the captain Shinonomi for more information. There is also a team coaching budget provided by the manager Gatt, allowing us to hire experienced and high level coaches for coaching sessions.

VOD review sessions of AOE matches will often occur on Sunday nights. All coaching feedback will be recorded into google documents which will be linked onto this page for easy access.
